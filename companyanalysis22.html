Posted on MARCH 21, 2021 BY STOCKIEST91


MARKET ADDED MORE THAN 1,000 POINTS IN THE LAST WEEK – WHERE MARKET IS HEADING NOW?


As we have seen that market rebounded from the bottom and closed in a positive territory with more than 1,000 points in outgoing week. From there market may likely take bit pressure of rising Covid_19 cases in across the Pakistan and things are on table for either implement Smart Lockdown once again to curb the rising pressure on economy.


Technical Stance | Market immediate support is hovering near 44,100 zone while resistance is just ahead at 45,000 (which is key level for momentum shift). Market holds the low level of 44,104 and rebounded however, that rebound was not sufficient enough for Bulls to control market – For bulls aggressive entry likely to expect after crossing the level of 45,600 (with sustainability). Volumes front quite good – consistent volumes generation keeps participation intact in market. In case of adverse move amid Covid-19 cases surging once again and market loose bullish momentum by breaking down the level of 42,500 that would not be good at all for market participants we can expect more downside on cards then, key benchmark index anyhow hold the level of 42,500 and you can place your logical stop loss near to that area.
