WEEKLY STRATEGY REPORT – Index Likely to Rebound and Upside Target 45,000


We believe that market will halt the downtrend and likely to push up index up-to our targeted level. 


Market showed a tremendous  rebound on the last trading session where index jacked up by 1,008.32 (2.36%) with volumes up-to 170mn shares whereas overall market volumes remained at 442.522mn shares.


During the session index tested a low of 42,779.76 and high 43,986.18 respectively.


Top traded companies which were remained in limelight – UNITY (88.257mn shares) followed by TRG (38.841mn shares), JSCL (17.736mn shares), PRL (17.390mn shares) and TELE (16.917mn shares).


TECHNICAL STANCE | temporary resistance stands at 43,900 – 44,000 zone. That’s a slight hurdle as of now, once market broken this level and start trading above then there would not be any major resistance before 45,000 zone in our view. The way market rebounded last day – clearly signal that market took support and really to fly up. After massive decline – from 47,300 to 42,689 index lost -9.74% therefore rebound highly likely expected at this level. If you go through our previous report there we quoted that start accumulation in the market. Currently index is trading at 43,788.08 and possible support should be at 42,600 (closing basis – stoploss level).
