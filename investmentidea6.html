We have reviewed the most tradeable companies of “Pakistan Stock Exchange”.


Market continued its downtrend – however we believe this would likely be ended soon…


As we have seen that market showed continuous weakness and crawling down to 43,548.21 zone. KSE100 face dent of -752.74 points in today’s session.


We believe that market would rebound from 43,500 level and give closing above 43,700, if that’s the case then you need to go long and hold your positions which will give you good amount of return in a very short span of time.


Majority of the stocks were heavily corrected now, its time to rebound – if we look at the volume profile then things are bit clear to us. Declining market with lower volumes indicating just a panic sell-off rather than actual sell-off. Therefore, its time to gradually build your long position and hold tightly. Few items are quite risky however, very lucrative for future i.e. TRG, UNITY, LUCK, GHNI, SAZEW, FFL, LOTCHEM, DOL, DGKC, ISL, MLCF and FCCL.


These above mentioned items are looking attractive at current rates for long-term investment for next 3 – 5 years period – will provide you handsome amount of return.
