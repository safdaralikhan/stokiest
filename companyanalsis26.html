DAILY REPORT – PSX & COMPANIES ANALYSIS | 26th March 2021


Posted on MARCH 25, 2021 BY STOCKIEST91


Key benchmark index closed at 45,726.16 by gaining 181.96 (0.40%) points in a session.


As we expected the resistance area near 45,800 level would be more stronger now.


We have witnessed strong rally in market post-opening bell which pushed market to the highest level of the day 46,000.11 zone. However, 46,000 was not sustainable and it came down to 45,726.16 area. During the session index tested a low of 45,544.20 which was last day closing level.


Overall market volumes clocked in at 470mn shares out of which 293mn shares. During the session, major traded companies which were in limelight – PTC (61.352mn shares) followed by TRG (39.010mn shares), BYCO (38.401mn shares), UNITY (28.586mn shares) and HASCOL (22.778mn shares).


Major positive points contribution came from TRG which added 107.27 ppts followed by ANL 20.91 ppts, LUCK 17.14 ppts, PAKT 13.62 ppts and KAPCO 12.66 ppts.


Technical Stance | Index has showed a profit-taking in the 2nd half of the market. Where index came down from 46,000 to 45,726.16 zone. Which is showing that entire gains were wiped out by the sellers. On technical chart, candle is not indicating bullish move likely to remain intact due to Market participants are not ready take more risk from the current level – where market can move in either way. In case that, index trade below 45,645 then you should place your stop loss below this level to avoid any miss trade due to urgency. As we heard about the outcome of FATF rating. Further, IMF is all set to ready $2bn loan in terms of extended funds facility (EFF). Although IMF impose restrictions as well which will dent on economy severely. We believe that “Sell on Strength” that’s a most wise able decision.


Companies Analysis – Trading Items | 26th March 2021


We have reviewed the most tradeable companies of “Pakistan Stock Exchange”.


TRG | We may expect a bit correction after two consecutive green session. Current price 165.68 – if trade below 165.68 then immediately sell position would be beneficial otherwise you can hold your long position. We suggest book gradual profit in TRG holding.


ISL | don’t make your long position in this stock. Wait for correction then you can enter into this stock near 85 zone. Although if selling pressure continues then it will break the level of 85 and down further to 83 level.


PSMC | has broken the 294 zone in today’s session therefore keep an eye and get entered into a long trade with immediate stop loss below 294 zone.


ATRL | gradually buying exhausting from this stock. Therefore, don’t go for buying unless its tested a support 244 – 245 level. Currently trading at 258.49 zone.


PSO | long entry only when its trading above from 244 zone. Otherwise, momentum will be slow. Downside re-buy area near 230 – 233 zone.


HASCOL | major support near 10.50 zone – if sustain this level then you can continue with your long position and hold this stock tightly in your hand. We are pitching this stock for next 6 – 8 months period and target would be in range of 20 – 22 OR 23.


LUCK | Momentum is getting flatter in this company, unless breakout above from 857 zone, don’t rush for buying this company’s share. We needs closing above from said level in order to confirm that bulls are still intact with this company. Current price level is 844.07 (consolidating and didn’t break previous recent high).


SHEL | consolidating at current level (162.94) – try to get gradual entry into this stock. Upside potential is huge for this company. (We are working on fundamental side to understand ground story about the company).


PRL | don’t rush to buy this stock. We are expecting a momentum shift is likely to come in this company.
