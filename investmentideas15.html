DAILY REPORT, KSE100 INDEX

Trading Calls / Investment Ideas | 15th April 2021


Posted on APRAIL 14, 2021 BY STOCKIEST91


Pakistan Stock Exchange, key benchmark index showed a positive move in the preceding day. This uptrend rally likely to continue – slightly resistance near 45,600 – 700 zone.


As we have seen the market rally (14-04-2021). Index was on buoyant note right after the opening bell. Major support to move the index were TRG which added 31.74 ppts standalone basis followed by FFC 19.16 ppts & HBL 18.72 ppts.


INDEX SUPPORT LEVEL WOULD BE NEAR 44,800 (STRONG SUPPORT). WHEREAS RESISTANCE WOULD BE NEAR 45,600 – 800.


However, market needs volumes – although we know that share activity will remain dull throughout holy month of Ramzan, still index needs a strong push from the bottom with volumetric support which rejuvenate the buying spree into market with more aggression.

SNGP | likely to hit 44 level soon. Price action enough strong to push price up. Since we are observing this stock, we saw that rang in which its trading from 38 – 42 zone.
