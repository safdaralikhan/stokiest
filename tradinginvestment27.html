DAILY REPORT, KSE100 INDEX

MARKET REPORT | TRADING & INVESTMENT IDEAS | 27-APRIL-2021


Posted on APRAIL 26, 2021 BY STOCKIEST91


MARKET OPENED ON A DULL NOTE – WHILE IN THE HOURLY HOUR MARKET RECOVERED MASSIVELY. INDEX TREND RESISTANCE ZONE


At bourse, we have witnessed extremely heavy buying pressure which recovered key benchmark index from the day low of 44,389.68 to the high of the day 45,952.45. Eventually market settled at 45,682.77 with gains extended by 976.01 (2.18%) in a single day.


Overall market volumes remained at 409.052mn shares, whereas KSE100 index volumes stood at 204.065mn shares.


Top traded companies were TELE (55.047mn shares) followed by TRG (53.626mn shares), UNITY (26.430mn shares) and WTL (21.059mn shares).

TECHNICAL STANCE – market immediate resistance hovering near 45,700 – 45,900 zone, however, most strongest resistance in our view near 46,000 – 46,300 zone. Today’s bulls attack on market would definitely build a buying momentum for couple of sessions  where market was down in last three straight sessions. Therefore, investors/traders were eager to hunt any opportunity which they like most. Though all the big companies were added positive points OGDC, PPL, HUBC, TRG, LUCK, HBL & PSO. These companies cumulatively added 499.09 points in a day.


Immediate support resides near 45,100 – 45,250 zone where from index likely to bounce back in case of decline.

We have reviewed the most tradeable companies of “Pakistan Stock Exchange”.

LUCK | trading at the verge of breakout from the current level therefore we would like to request you to watch this stock and if any kind of weakness observe then go for a short in intraday basis – any weakness will lead to decline till 850 – 852 zone while currently stock trading at 867.77 so alot of room to make money. Momentum quite strong at present level.


TELE | formed strong bearish engulfing pattern on chart – therefore no fresh buying in coming sessions.

GGL | such like driving by unbreakable bulls. Stock has rallied more than 24% since Feb 2021. Currently trading at 37.63. Further upside likely to expect therefore you can make an entry till this stock is trading above 33.60 zone (closing basis).


HASCOL | ONLY ACCUMULATION STRATEGY WOULD BE USED FOR THIS COMPANY. WE ARE EYEING THAT THIS GOING TO BOUNCE BACK SOON.
