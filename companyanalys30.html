DAILY REPORT, KSE100 INDEX

Companies Analysis | 30th March 2021


Posted on MARCH 29, 2021 BY STOCKIEST91


We have reviewed the most tradeable companies of “Pakistan Stock Exchange”.


Market will likely to give short pull-back during intraday time where you can hunt opportunities for a short period of time.


LUCK | As we informed in our previous report about the downtrend, now LUCK is trading at 820. Keep your eyes on it, key area for rebound 790 – 800 in between where you can build long position and hold your position for an upside upto 840 – 845 level.

TRG | another hot scrip of our market hit badly by bears. Formed a big bearish candle which is indicating that going forward trend will continue to be sluggish. However, if sustain the lower level of 154.16 then we can expect a sharp rebound in next 1 – 2 days upto 160 – 163 once again.


ISL | keep your eyes on it, trading near support area. Therefore anytime rebound expected if sustain the level of 82 – 83 zone. Upside will be attractive with current rate – expected upside would be 88 – 90 level.


GHNI | this stock needs closing above 241 level in order to attract bulls into this stock. Once bulls comes then immediate upside would be 250 – 255 in very short span of time. Otherwise it will further decline to 210 level.


TRPOL | We believe that now its time to gradually off-load the holding position in this stock. This stock already rallied massively and now its time to get some rest. Currently trading at 14.02 while today’s high was 14.95. Huge upside price rejection give some signal of halting aggressive uptrend in this stock. Due to not very much fundamental story – possible chances this stock will come down to back to level where from started.


LOTCHEM | don’t be excited in this company, false breakout observed due to level was not sustained above 15.55 and today’s closing 15.30 level. Therefore, immediate resistance now 15.55 level – if crosses above then we can re-think for entry otherwise just sell on strength and wait for downside 14.


INIL | currently trading in the downtrend channel. Immediate upside resistance near 204 – 205 zone. If resists there then it will further come down to 179 – 182 level. Therefore, be cautious in long position.


FATIMA | trading in the consolidation phase therefore avoid trading in this company. Consolidation range 27 – 30 level.


EFERT | trading near support level 62 – therefore we believe that put this company on your radar in order to hunt good opportunity. Initial upside target would be 66 with stop loss below 60 (closing basis).
